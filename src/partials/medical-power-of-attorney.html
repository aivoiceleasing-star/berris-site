Medical Power of Attorney

A medical power of attorney, called a patient advocate designation in Michigan, names someone you trust to make healthcare decisions if you cannot speak for yourself.

Paired with a living will, it makes sure your wishes are known and followed, and it spares your family from guessing during a medical crisis.

What this covers

  • ✓Name a patient advocate you trust
  • ✓Put your healthcare wishes in writing
  • ✓Guide decisions about treatment and end-of-life care
  • ✓Spare your family from having to guess
