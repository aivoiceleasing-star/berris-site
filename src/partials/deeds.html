Deeds and Lady Bird Deeds

The right deed keeps your Michigan real estate moving the way you intend. We prepare legally sound, customized deeds for transfers, ownership changes, and estate planning.

That includes Lady Bird deeds, also called enhanced life estate deeds, which can pass your home to your family outside of probate while you keep full control during your lifetime.

What this covers

  • ✓Transfer property cleanly and correctly
  • ✓Use a Lady Bird deed to avoid probate on your home
  • ✓Add or remove owners on a deed
  • ✓Coordinate the deed with your trust or will

A Lady Bird deed is a Michigan planning tool. We will tell you whether it fits your situation or whether a trust is the better path.
