Guardianship

Guardianship arrangements make sure the people who depend on you are cared for by someone you choose. For parents, that means naming a guardian for minor children in your will.

For families caring for an adult with special needs or an aging parent, we help establish the right guardianship or conservatorship, and we coordinate it with the rest of your plan.

What this covers

  • ✓Name a guardian for minor children
  • ✓Establish guardianship for an adult who cannot manage their own affairs
  • ✓Coordinate guardianship with your will and trust
  • ✓Reduce the chance of a contested court process
