Trust Packages

A revocable living trust lets your estate pass to the people you choose without going through probate. That means privacy, a faster process, and lower settlement costs for your family, while you keep full control of everything during your lifetime.

Our trust packages include the trust itself along with the supporting documents that make it work, so your plan is complete and ready to fund.

What this covers

  • ✓Avoid probate on assets held in the trust
  • ✓Keep your affairs private, since a trust is not part of the public record
  • ✓Control how and when your beneficiaries receive their inheritance
  • ✓Plan for incapacity without a court-appointed conservator

A revocable living trust is designed to avoid probate and preserve privacy and control. It does not shield assets from your own creditors during your lifetime.
