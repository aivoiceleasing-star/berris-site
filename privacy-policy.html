Privacy Policy

Berris Law Firm, PLLC ("we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or interact with our services. By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information

We Collect We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and case-related details submitted through forms or consultations. When you visit our website, we may also automatically collect your IP address, browser and device information, pages viewed, interaction data, and cookies and tracking technologies. This information helps us improve the functionality and performance of our website.

2. How

We Use Your Information We may use your information to respond to inquiries and provide legal services, communicate with you regarding your request or case, improve our website and user experience, maintain internal records and compliance, and send updates related to your communication preferences. We do not sell your personal information.

3. SMS

and Text Messaging Policy If you opt in to receive text messages from us, you may receive updates related to your inquiry, appointments, or services. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

We Share Information We may share information with service providers who assist in operations, legal professionals or entities involved in a case when relevant and with your consent, and authorities if required by law.
