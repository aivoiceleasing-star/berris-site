The Berris process

Four meetings, one finished plan

  1. 1

    BLF Family Legacy Planning Session

    We sit down, learn your family and your goals, and map what a plan needs to do. No legalese, no pressure.

  2. 2

    BLF Design Meeting

    We choose the right structure together, wills or trusts, and you see the flat fee before any work begins.

  3. 3

    Review Process

    You read every document in plain language. We revise until it says exactly what you want it to say.

  4. 4

    Signing Ceremony

    We sign, witness, and notarize everything correctly, so the plan holds up when your family needs it.

Without a plan, Michigan law writes one for you.

Probate is public, slow, and costly, and the state decides who gets what. A plan put in place now keeps those decisions in your hands and spares your family the hardest version of a hard day.
